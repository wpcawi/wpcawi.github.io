Training & Education

Our apprenticeship training and continuing journeymen education programs are regarded to be among the finest in the nation. Our members are committed to safe worksites, quality workmanship and cost effective services for our customers.

Your commercial or industrial facility depends strongly on your essential machinery working for you. That equipment can represent a huge percentage of your business investment. When considering all the aspects of your equipment –from harsh environment, extreme temperatures, daily operational wear and tear, including the vast range of moving parts – you need professionals in protective coatings that will skillfully prolong the functionality and longevity of your equipment.

Your industry may require very specialized coatings for your equipment. WPCA members are highly skilled professionals trained for all aspects of coating requirements. Committed to excellence, WPCA members start each project with close and detailed project planning and evaluate the scope of work necessary to complete the project. All aspects are closely reviewed and your project is then strategically designed to meet your needs while preventing interruption to your production and staying within your budget.
