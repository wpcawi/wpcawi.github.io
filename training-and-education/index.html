    Training & Education

    Our apprenticeship training and continuing journeymen education programs are regarded to be among the finest in the nation. Our members are committed to safe worksites, quality workmanship and cost effective services for our customers.

    Each commercial or industrial facility depends strongly on the essential machinery working efficiently. That equipment can represent a huge percentage of business investment. When considering all of the aspects the equipment is exposed to – from harsh environment, extreme temperatures, daily operational wear and tear and the vast range of moving parts – hiring experienced professionals knowledgeable in protective coatings that will skillfully prolong the functionality and longevity of the production equipment is essential.

    Each industry requires very specialized coatings in order to maintain and protect the specific equipment. WPCA members are highly skilled professionals trained for all aspects of coating requirements. Committed to excellence, WPCA members start each project with close and detailed project planning and evaluate the scope of work necessary to complete the project. All aspects of the project are closely reviewed; and coating selections are strategically chosen to meet project requirements while minimizing interruption to production and staying within budget.
