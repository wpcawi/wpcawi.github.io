    Drug Program

    In the construction industry, safety is a huge concern of both contractors and employees. Impaired judgement can cause serious on-site problems. WPCA members participate in a Substance Abuse Testing and Assistance Program to increase job site safety.

    The goals of the program are to create a drug-free and alcohol-free workplace by:

    • • Creating a safe and healthy working environment for all employees;
    • • Reducing substance abuse related injuries and tardiness; and
    • • Providing rehabilitation for employees who seek help

    Employees of WPCA contractors are subject to pre-employment screenings, reasonable suspicion testing, and post-accident testing. This program helps to insure a safe work place for all.
