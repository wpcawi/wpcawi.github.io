    Safety focused, relationship based and women owned. We look and treat each project individually as each is unique and important to us. We are passionate about preserving, protecting and extending the lives of equipment, structures, buildings and anything subject to rust, corrosion or environmental conditions. Our customers’ businesses will operate stronger when their assets “live” longer.

    Since 1979 Porta-Painting has been serving the industrial painting and commercial painting markets in Wisconsin. Over the past 38 years, we have developed a solid reputation for working safely and providing high quality results. By offering individualized solutions with ethical business practices, Porta-Painting has continued to be many companies’ top choice painting partner.
