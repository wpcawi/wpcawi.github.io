    Safety On The Job

    Safety training and safety awareness are top priorities. Reducing the risk of injuries and illness enables the project to run smoothly and efficiently. Injuries on the job create significant delays and loss in productivity. The negative consequences can be costly to the company by tarnishing the companies reputation and losing customer satisfaction. Implementing a strong injury and illness prevention program is effective in reducing incidents and will significantly improve on site safety.

    OSHA has defined construction workers “fatal Four” fatalities as:

    • Falls

    • Struck By Object

    • Electrocutions

    • Caught In/Between
